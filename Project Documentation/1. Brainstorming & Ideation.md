# Brainstorming & Ideation

## Introduction
Rising Waters is an AI-based flood prediction system developed to predict flood risks using environmental and historical data. The main idea is to use Machine Learning techniques to analyze different factors responsible for floods and provide early predictions.

## Problem Identification
Floods are major natural disasters that cause damage to human lives, agriculture, infrastructure, and the environment. Traditional flood monitoring systems may not always provide accurate and timely predictions. An intelligent prediction system is required to identify possible flood situations in advance.

## Idea Generation
The project idea was generated by considering the need for an automated flood prediction system. Machine Learning algorithms can analyze large amounts of environmental data and identify patterns that help in predicting flood conditions.

## Proposed Solution
The proposed solution is an AI-powered flood prediction model that takes input parameters such as rainfall, water level, temperature, and other environmental factors to predict flood possibilities.

## Objectives
- To develop a machine learning-based flood prediction system.
- To analyze environmental data for identifying flood risks.
- To provide accurate and quick flood predictions.
- To support disaster management and early warning systems.
